Visit the Energy Expo page

This is an opportunity for holistic health practitioners, metaphysicians and various other healers to exhibit their special talents, gifts and skills. There will be space for over 40 exhibitors and two full days of workshops (14 in total) at grassroots prices: early bird exhibition spaces for as low as $75 and $10 admission fee for those wanting to attend this special experience. A portion of the proceeds will be going to the Fernwood:NRG to help support low income housing and community activities, to find out more visit www.fernwoodneighbourhood.ca.
